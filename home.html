ABOUT US

Founded upon owners Korben Merckx, Jonas Vranken, Sofie Daelemans and Tessa Segers enduring drive to find great bands and get their music out to as many people as possible. Alphaglam has brought wave after wave of powerful, innovative, and often genre-defining music to the ever-hungry glam metal masses. It is this ethos that has seen Alphaglam build op a stunning and diverse catalogue, weather the various storms facing any independent label.

OUR VISION

We want the vibes from the early 80's back. We all guarantee the same authenticity as in the old days.

TIMELESS

We are in it for the long term. All the groups and song are meant to last forever. Reviving is our basic.

QUALITY

With the durability of our LP's you'll be able to listen to a wonderfull sound, not only now but also in the future.
